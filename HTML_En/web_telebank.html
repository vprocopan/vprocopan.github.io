With the Internet-banking "Telebank.md" using your web browser options you:

  • To view the status of your accounts and make transfers to them
  • To make instant exchange
  • To make transfers and payments
  • To pay for mobile communications, internet, utilities, etc.
  • To pay taxes, fines and government service agencies
  • To view information about all the plastic cards
  • To relocate and fill deposit accounts online
  • To repay loans
  • Patterns
  • Thus, once a pattern in fulfilling Telebank.md system, you'll be issued with sheets of accounts needs. The registered template also possible, to arrange an automatic payment of your accounts and services (money orders Autoefectuarea).

Telebank.md - is simple and quick to connect, no need to install special software and features a user-friendly interface.
