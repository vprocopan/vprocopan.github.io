Payments and Transfers
Payment arrangements, passed by Client to the Bank till 16:30, are fulfilled by the current day of the Bank. Because of possible daily rate of exchange changes, buying or selling of the currency arrangements passed to the Bank after 16:30, are not executed.

Card account replenishment order, passed by Client to the Bank till 16:30, are fulfilled by the current day of the Bank. Card account replenishment order, passed to the Bank after 16:30, are fulfilled by the next Bank day. After the replenishment of a card account order through "Telebank" system, the resources will be accessible not earlier than after 2 hours, after it was executed by the Bank.

Currency buying
This menu offers buying currency, by transferring money from an account in MDL to another account with different type of currency, applying the exchange rate of BC EuroCreditBank SA.

To make an exchange, select MDL account, type the sum you want to exchange (field: in sum), other fields are automatically filled in, which include information like: exchange rate, and the planned and current balance. Press Save to perform an exchange, or Cancel

Columns Current balance and Planned balance are automatically calculated for both the accounts.

Currency selling
This menu offers buying currency, by transferring money from an account in foreign currency to another account with MDL, applying the current exchange rate of BC EuroCreditBank SA.

To make an exchange, select MDL account, type the sum you want to exchange (field: in sum), other fields are automatically filled in, which include information like: exchange rate, and the planned and current balance. Press Save to perform an exchange, or Cancel

Columns Current balance and Planned balance are automatically calculated for the both used accounts.
